Grading Information

Grading Scale

Final Grade Percent Range

A 89.5 - 100%

B 79.5 - 89.4%

C 69.5 - 79.4%

D 59.5 - 69.4%

F* Less than 59.5%

*Automatic Failure

  • A failing grade on the final project
  • A failing grade on class attendance
  • A violation of the University Academic Honesty Policy
Course Evaluation Process
  • 40% dev projects.
    four development projects
  • 20% exams.
    two in-class examinations, mid-term (10%) & final (10%)
  • 20% participation.
    attendance, class activities, labs, study quizzes, pop quizzes
  • 20% homework.
    CodeAcademy courses
Exceptions

Considered on a case-by-case basis and require pre-approval

Grade Disputes
  • Please submit possible grading errors or disputes via Canvas Inbox within one week of the grade posting.
  • Please include all pertinent information including the specific assignment, question number & text, instructions, textbook reference, screenshot, or other.
  • If you do include a screenshot, also transcribe any information into text.
  • Do not submit via submission comments.
